Chatsen

Chat on the go

Chatsen is a Twitch chat application for iOS and Android with support for 7TV, BTTV and FFZ.

iOS Android

Features

Have it your way

With a various selection of colors and other customizable options, you are free to configure Chatsen the way you prefer! Dark and light theme included.

It's got it all

With support for 7TV, BTTV, and FFZ, you will never have to see any emote as text in chat! Search for emotes and browse what the channels you visit have at their disposal.

Never miss out on anything

Join up to 4500 channels at the same time and get notified when you get mentioned or receive a whisper. We've even got a mentions menu to help you not miss important messages!

Watch your favorite streamers live on the go

With a built-in video player, you can watch a streamer and chat in multiple channels simultaneously!
